About Me

Hello! My name is Lanae Foard and after spending 10 years as an environmental professional, and most of my life in Texas, I am starting over in a new place. And where better to start over than Tucson, Arizona? I moved here for the warm winters and the mountain views.

My passions are my family, my pets, the outdoors, and travel. I plan to work while I travel the continental U.S. in my travel trailer-home.
